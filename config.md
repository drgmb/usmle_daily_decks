### USMLE Daily Decks - Configuration

#### error_tag
Your personal error/weak-point tag used to filter cards.
- **Default**: `"gmberro"`
- Change this to your own tag (e.g., `"myerrors"`, `"weak"`)

#### auto_rebuild
Enable automatic daily deck rebuild when Anki starts.
- **Default**: `false`
- Set to `true` to automatically rebuild filtered decks each day

#### last_build_day
Internal field tracking the last build day. Do not modify manually.
- **Default**: `-1`
